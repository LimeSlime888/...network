## 1.1. griefing
1. you MUST NOT overwrite with redundant or meaningless text:
1.1. artwork
1.2. meaningful and nonredundant text
1.3. tiles within |tileX \* tileY| ≤ 32
1.4. roads not within 2.1.1.3
2. violations of 2.1.1 will have their effects reverted as soon as possible if the revert does not break 2.1.1
3. if a person is known to have violated 2.1.1, they SHOULD be punished under an Operator's discretion
4. 2.1.1 does not apply within the purge limits during a purge
5. 2.1.1.1~2 do not apply if the content violates 1.1.?
## 2.2. regenerating structures
1. a Regenerating Structure is a collection of characters that are regenerated at a rate of at most completely per 24 hours
2. you MUST NOT reinforce Regenerating Structures that cover at most 4 tiles for:
2.1. more than 16 in 72 hours within 4 tiles of spawn
2.2. more than 36 in 72 hours within 10 tiles of spawn
3. you MUST NOT reinforce Regenerating Structures that cover 5~16 tiles for:
3.1. within 4 tiles of spawn
3.2. more than 16 in 72 hours within 10 tiles of spawn
3.3. more than 36 in 72 hours within 20 tiles of spawn
4. you MUST NOT reinforce Regenerating Structures that cover 17~36 tiles for:
4.1. within 10 tiles of spawn
4.2. more than 16 in 72 hours within 20 tiles of spawn
4.3. more than 36 in 72 hours within 32 tiles of spawn
5. for 2.2.2~5, "within n tiles of spawn" means where the Regenerating Structure is within any tile where (tileX+.5)² + (tileY-.5)² ≤ n²
6. violations of 2.2.2~5 SHOULD be handled by protecting the area of the structure
7. if a person is known to have violated 2.2.2~5, they MAY be punished under an Operator's discretion
## 2.3. code of conduct
1. you MUST NOT communicate with the intention to impact a person negatively or incite anger
2. you MUST NOT harass or promote harassment of a person
3. you MUST NOT link personally identifying information to a person if the person intends to keep the information private. this includes, but is not limited to:
3.1. area of residence, when specified more precisely than country
3.2. legal name
3.3. date of birth or age, when specified more precisely than 1 year
3.4. place of birth, when specified more precisely than country
4. violations of 2.1.1~2 SHOULD be handled by informing the perpetrator of their violation and to take steps to avoid violation
5. if a person is known to have violated 2.1.1~3, they MAY be punished under an Operator's discretion
