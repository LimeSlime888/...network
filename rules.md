# 2. the canvas of ...world
the following apply to the canvas of [/...world](https://owot.me/...world), the intended centre of interaction of ...network.
## 2.0. dictionary
1. *within n tiles of spawn* means within any tile where (tileX+.5)² + (tileY-.5)² ≤ n²
2. *road* means a linear structure that spans 128 tiles or more measured at its widest and 4 tiles or less measured at its thinnest
## 2.1. code of conduct
1. you MUST NOT communicate with the intention to impact someone negatively or incite anger
2. you MUST NOT promote or engage in harassment
	1. you MUST NOT promote violence, especially in discrimination towards a group of people
3. you MUST NOT attempt to impersonate someone
4. you MUST NOT threaten someone with violence or other actions intended to cause harm
5. you MUST NOT link personally identifying information to someone if they intend to keep the information private. this includes, but is not limited to:
    1. area of residence, when specified more precisely than country
    2. legal name
    3. date of birth or age, when specified more precisely than 1 year
    4. place of birth, when specified more precisely than country
6. you MUST NOT promote or normalise self-harm, pedophilia, or zoophilia
7. you MUST NOT promote or engage in financial scams or fraud
8. violations of 2.1.1~7 SHOULD be handled by informing the perpetrator of their violation and to take steps to avoid violation
9. violations of 2.1.5~7 MUST be handled by deleting the violating content as soon as possible
10. if a person is known to have violated 2.1.1~7, they MAY be punished under an Operator's discretion
## 2.2. griefing
1. you MUST NOT overwrite with redundant or meaningless text:
    1. artwork
    2. meaningful and nonredundant text
    3. tiles within |tileX \* tileY| ≤ 32
    4. roads not within 2.2.1.iii
3. violations of 2.2.1 will have their effects reverted as soon as possible if the revert does not break 2.2.1
4. if a person is known to have violated 2.2.1, they SHOULD be punished under an Operator's discretion
5. 2.2.1 does not apply within the purge limits during a purge
6. 2.2.1.i~ii do not apply if the content violates other rules
## 2.3. regenerating structures
1. a Regenerating Structure is a collection of characters that are regenerated at a rate of at most completely per 24 hours
2. you MUST NOT reinforce Regenerating Structures that cover at most 4 tiles for:
    1. more than 16 in 72 hours within 4 tiles of spawn
    2. more than 36 in 72 hours within 10 tiles of spawn
3. you MUST NOT reinforce Regenerating Structures that cover 5~16 tiles for:
    1. within 4 tiles of spawn
    2. more than 16 in 72 hours within 10 tiles of spawn
    3. more than 36 in 72 hours within 20 tiles of spawn
4. you MUST NOT reinforce Regenerating Structures that cover 17~36 tiles for:
    1. within 10 tiles of spawn
    2. more than 16 in 72 hours within 20 tiles of spawn
    3. more than 36 in 72 hours within 32 tiles of spawn
5. violations of 2.3.2~5 SHOULD be handled by protecting the area of the structure
6. if a person is known to have violated 2.3.2~5, they MAY be punished under an Operator's discretion
## 2.4. prohibited content
1. you MUST NOT link to or write sexually explicit content at any location in the canvas
2. you MUST NOT link to or write sexually suggestive content to the canvas within 4096 tiles of spawn or within 32 tiles of a road
3. you MUST NOT depict graphic violence
4. violations of 2.4.1,3 MUST be handled by deleting the violating content as soon as possible
5. violations of 2.4.2 MUST be handled by moving the violating content outside of where 2.4.2 applies as soon as possible
